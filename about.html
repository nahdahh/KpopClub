Our Story

K-pop Club started in 2020 as a small online community of passionate K-pop fans who wanted to share their love for Korean music and culture. What began as a simple blog quickly evolved into a thriving marketplace for authentic K-pop merchandise.

Founded by a group of friends who bonded over their shared love for groups like NewJeans, BTS, Blackpink, and Twice, we've grown into a global community connecting fans from all over the world.

Our mission is to bring authentic K-pop merchandise to fans while creating a space where the K-pop community can connect, share, and celebrate together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products authentic?

Yes! All our products are 100% authentic and sourced directly from official suppliers in South Korea. We never sell counterfeit items.

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping (US) typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days depending on your location and customs processing.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging. For exchanges, please contact our customer support team.

How can I join the K-pop Club community?

You can join our community by signing up for an account on our website, following us on social media, and participating in our forums and events!

Do you host K-pop events?

Yes! We regularly host viewing parties, album unboxings, dance workshops, and fan meetups. Check our events page or follow us on social media for updates.
