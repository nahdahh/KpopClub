BoyNextDoor Announces Summer Comeback with Mini Album "NOGENRE"

May 10, 2025
KPOP CLUB Editor
1,234 views
3 min read

BoyNextDoor is set to make their highly anticipated comeback this summer with their new mini album "NOGENRE". Pre-orders start next week!

BoyNextDoor has officially announced their summer comeback with the release of their highly anticipated mini album titled "NOGENRE". The announcement came through their official social media channels, sending fans into a frenzy of excitement.

Album Details

The mini album "NOGENRE" will feature 6 tracks, including the title track "Summer Nights" and several B-side tracks that showcase the group's versatility and growth as artists.

Pre-Order Information

Pre-orders for "NOGENRE" will begin on May 15, 2025, and will be available through KPOP CLUB official store and major online retailers worldwide.

The album will be available in three different versions: Day, Night, and Special Edition. Each version will include exclusive photocards and posters.

Stay tuned to KPOP CLUB for more updates on BoyNextDoor's comeback and pre-order information!
